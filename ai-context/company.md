# EventNestGroup — Company Overview

## Identity
- **Name**: EventNestGroup
- **Domain**: eventnestgroup.com
- **Tagline**: The Smarter Way to Plan Events
- **Type**: Two-sided marketplace for event services
- **Stage**: Early-stage, launched product, growing vendor and customer base
- **Geography**: UK and Ireland (primary), expanding to English-speaking markets

## What We Do
EventNestGroup operates Event Nest, a platform that connects people planning events with professional event vendors. We eliminate the fragmented, stressful process of finding and booking vendors by putting discovery, comparison, messaging, booking, and reviews in one place — with AI-powered planning assistance.

## Mission
Make event planning simple, transparent, and enjoyable for everyone — from a first-time birthday party planner to a seasoned wedding organiser.

## Company Values
- **Transparency**: Real prices, verified reviews, no hidden fees
- **Simplicity**: One platform replaces ten apps, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp groups
- **Fairness**: Free for customers. Vendors only pay when they win business.
- **Trust**: Every vendor is vetted. Every review comes from a real booking.
- **Innovation**: AI-powered planning tools that no competitor offers

## Team Context
- Solo-founder / small team operation
- Technical founder — the product is built and maintained in-house
- No dedicated marketing team — AI tools and automation fill this gap
- Community-driven growth through vendor referrals and customer word-of-mouth

## Brand Voice
- Helpful and warm, not corporate
- Confident but not pushy
- Modern and approachable
- Speaks plainly — no jargon or marketing fluff
- Celebrates the joy of events, not the stress of planning

## Key Dates & Seasonality
- **Wedding season**: April–September (peak demand)
- **Christmas party season**: October–December
- **Graduation/prom**: May–July
- **Festival season**: June–August
- **New Year planning**: January–February (resolution-driven event planning)
- Vendor acquisition should run year-round; customer marketing intensifies before peak seasons
