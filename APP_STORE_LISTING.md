## Description (4000 chars max)

Event Nest makes it easy to find and book trusted event vendors for any occasion. Whether you're planning a wedding, birthday party, corporate event, or baby shower, browse hundreds of verified professionals — photographers, caterers, DJs, florists, decorators and more.

DISCOVER VENDORS
Browse a curated marketplace of event professionals. Filter by category, location, price, and rating. View portfolios, read verified reviews, and compare vendors side by side.

AI EVENT PLANNER
Not sure where to start? Our AI-powered event planner creates a personalised plan for your event — complete with vendor recommendations, budget breakdown, timeline, and a planning checklist.

BOOK WITH CONFIDENCE
Send enquiries, request custom quotes, and manage your bookings all in one place. Chat directly with vendors, negotiate pricing, and confirm dates — no phone tag required.

STAY ORGANISED
Track your bookings, manage your event checklist, save favourite vendors to wishlists, and get reminders as your event approaches. Everything you need in one app.

FOR VENDORS
List your services, showcase your portfolio, receive booking enquiries, send custom quotes, and grow your business. Manage your calendar, track analytics, and build your reputation with verified reviews.

KEY FEATURES
- Browse vendors by category, location, and price
- AI-powered event planning with personalised recommendations
- Direct messaging with vendors
- Custom quote requests and negotiations
- Booking management and calendar sync
- Vendor wishlists and comparison
- Push notifications for bookings, messages, and updates
- Event planning checklists with timeline tracking
- Verified vendor reviews with photos
- Works across the UK, Ireland, and beyond
