## Sheet Structure

### Tab 1: Social Posts

**Column Headers:**
| A | B | C | D | E | F | G | H |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Date** | **Platform** | **Content Type** | **Post Text** | **Canva Brief** | **Hashtags** | **Status** | **Notes** |

**Column Details:**
- **A - Date**: When to schedule (e.g., "2026-03-18")
- **B - Platform**: Instagram / Facebook / LinkedIn / TikTok
- **C - Content Type**: Vendor Spotlight / Tip / Success Story / Feature / Testimonial
- **D - Post Text**: The actual caption/copy for the post
- **E - Canva Brief**: Design instructions (e.g., "Instagram post 1080x1350px: vendor photo + quote overlay + Event Nest logo")
- **F - Hashtags**: #EventNest #Vendors #Photography (platform-specific)
- **G - Status**: "Draft" → review → "✓ Approved" or "⚠️ Change X"
- **H - Notes**: Team comments / revision requests

**Example Row:**
```
2026-03-18 | Instagram | Vendor Spotlight | "Meet Sarah, our top photographer 📸 50+ bookings in 2 months! Start earning on Event Nest today" | Instagram: 1080x1350px, Sarah's best photo, add testimonial quote, purple accent | #EventNest #Photographers #WeddingPhotography #HirePhotographer | Draft | Looks good, use the sunset photo
```

## Approval Workflow

1. **I generate content** (weekly) and paste into "Draft" rows
2. **You review** in the sheet (you + team can comment)
3. **Mark Status**:
   - ✓ Approved — ready to go
   - ⚠️ Change X — rewrite this part
   - ❌ Reject — don't use this one
4. **Add Notes** if you want revisions
5. **Tell me in chat**: "Approved all from 3/18" or list the ones to change
6. **I push approved posts** to Buffer + Canva briefs to you
